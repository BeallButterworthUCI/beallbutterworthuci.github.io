## Confirmed structure

- Two competitive tracks: **Butterworth Product Development** (ICS) and **Beall Student Design** (Engineering).
- An **International Collaboration Prize** continuing the partnership with the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), Brazil.
- Standard placement prizes: $10,000 / $6,500 / $3,500 per track.

## Adjacent macro events

- **Stacey Nicholas Dean Magnus Egerstedt** departed UCI to become Provost / EVC at UNC Chapel Hill effective March 2, 2026. **Prof. Faryar Jabbari** (MAE) became interim Dean effective March 1, 2026. A permanent-dean search committee chaired by Distinguished Prof. Lee Swindlehurst was announced May 4, 2026.
- UCI launched the **Engineering+ Sustainability Institute** on March 5, 2026, focused on critical materials (rare-earth extraction/recovery, sustainable ammonia, sustainable cement), funded by a $2.5M seed allocation (over 5 years) from the $50M Samueli gift for three Engineering+ institutes.
