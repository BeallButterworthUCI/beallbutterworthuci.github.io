## Notes

- **Qardiac &rarr; Vena Vitals** is the cleanest BBComp commercial trajectory on record. Co-founded 2019 by Joshua Kim (CTO), Ray Liu (CEO), and Prof. Michelle Khine. Y Combinator S20 batch. FDA clearance anticipated within 2026; new R&D lab opened at Roseman University of Nevada Summerlin on March 25, 2026.
- **CenSyn Inc.** went on to raise a $105K Berkeley SkyDeck Batch 12 cohort investment plus Arizona Commerce Authority funding; product is the **CenSyn PenEEG**; pursuing FDA 510(k).
- **AllerChain** (Aguas, McGrale, Shakya, Ananthakrishnan, Agarwal) was a 2019 combined-track finalist and returned in 2021 to win the Beall 1st with **forMED Technologies**.
