## Notes

- **Qardiac &rarr; Vena Vitals** is the cleanest BBComp commercial trajectory on record. Co-founded 2019 by Joshua Kim (CTO), Ray Liu (CEO) and Prof. Michelle Khine. Y Combinator S20 batch. FDA clearance anticipated within 2026. New R&amp;D lab opened at Roseman University of Nevada Summerlin on March 25, 2026.
- **CenSyn Inc.** went on to raise a $105K Berkeley SkyDeck Batch 12 cohort investment plus Arizona Commerce Authority funding. Product is the **CenSyn PenEEG**. Pursuing FDA 510(k). Anirudh "Andy" Bhushan, Trevor "Gray" Silence and Ayushi Patel are the active CenSyn co-founders post-graduation.
- **AllerChain** (Aguas, McGrale, Shakya, Ananthakrishnan, Agarwal) was a 2019 combined-track finalist and returned in 2021 to win the Beall 1st with **forMED Technologies**.
