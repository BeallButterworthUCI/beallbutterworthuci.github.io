<p class="lede">The 7th annual Butterworth Product Development Competition was held in 2010, but the legacy ICS news archive returns a 500-series error on the relevant article IDs, and Google's index does not surface the specific results.</p>

<p class="note"><strong>Help us recover the record:</strong> If you participated, judged, or mentored the 2010 cycle, or have a program booklet, please <a href="https://github.com/BeallButterworthUCI/History/issues">open an issue</a> or contribute via the <a href="https://github.com/BeallButterworthUCI/History/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md">archive</a>.</p>

## What we know

- The competition was held annually starting around 2003-2004. That places the 2010 cycle as the **7th annual Butterworth Product Development Competition**.
- Prize tier in this era: $5,000 / $3,500 / $1,500.
- Hosted by the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences. Single-track (software / ICS only).
- The 2003-2009 winners are similarly absent from the public record.
