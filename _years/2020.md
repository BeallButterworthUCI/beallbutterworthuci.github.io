<p class="lede">The 2020 cycle was cancelled outright due to the COVID-19 pandemic.</p>

The competition pivoted to virtual planning during the academic year and resumed with a fully virtual ceremony in 2021. The cancellation is the only break in the joint-edition era's annual cadence since 2014.

## Related events from 2020

- **Y Combinator Summer 2020 (S20):** Vena Vitals - the commercial follow-on of the 2019 Beall 1st-place Qardiac team - joined Y Combinator's S20 batch as a Khine-Lab spinoff co-founded by Ray Liu (CEO), Michelle Khine, and Josh Kim (CTO).
- **123 Baby Box:** Zarina Bahadur (2018 Butterworth 1st ZOOT team) won the **$25,000 Grand Prize** at the 2020 Stella Zhang New Venture Competition (Consumer Products 1st) - the first Master of Innovation and Entrepreneurship (MIE) student to win the NVC.
