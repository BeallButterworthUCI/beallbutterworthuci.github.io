## Notes

- 2017 was the last year at the original $7,500 / $5,000 / $2,500 prize tier.
- **Olaoluwa Adesanya's** Palmplug is one of the BBComp's most enduring commercial spinouts. His brother **Adelanwa Adesanya** (2017 combined-track finalist) went on to co-found <a href="https://www.movnhealth.com/">Moving Analytics / Movn Health</a> (telehealth cardiac rehab; 2018 Forbes 30 Under 30).
- **Wing Assistant** (Pippy follow-on) reports over 1,000 employees across 10 countries on Wing's own blog, founded directly by 2017 Pippy team members Karan Kanwar and Martin Gomez.
- Source: <a href="https://bbcomp.tech.uci.edu/past/2017-participants-and-results/">bbcomp.tech.uci.edu, 2017</a>.
