- **VeinGuard roster note:** the official BBComp 2025 winners page lists five members; the project site at <a href="http://veinguard.uci.design/">veinguard.uci.design</a> adds Raveena Atwal and Marlena Ramanis. We retain the seven-name roster from the project site.
