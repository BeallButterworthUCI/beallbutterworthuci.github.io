## Notes

- **Sentinel** went on to win the 2024 Stella Zhang NVC Grand Finale ($20K + $10K) and placed 2nd at the C&C Innovative Solutions SoCal Super Pitch &mdash; one of the strongest cross-competition records in BBComp history.
- All three top-tier joint-track winners (Sentinel, Two Cube, NeoMold) placed in the 2024 Stella Zhang NVC.
- **Spotter AI** (parent of Sentinel) is the Wilmington, DE trucking-AI dispatch company founded 2019. CEO and Co-Founder: Peidi Wu. Co-Founder, CTO and President: Gabriel Stonys.
- Director David Ochi noted the 2024 participant count "eclipsed any previous year by over 25%."
