## Notes

- **David Ochi** joined as Competition Director in 2014 and has remained in the role through 2026.
- **Prof. Michelle Khine** mentored both Spero Diagnostics (1st) and A Hundred Tiny Hands (2nd). Her lab later became the densest faculty-to-PhD pipeline in the BBComp archive - at least six BBComp competitors became Khine Lab PhD alumni (Sophia Lin, Eugene Lee, Jolie Nokes, Joshua Kim, Lancy Lin, Sophia E.F. Chou).
- The Khine Lab's "Shrinky Dink microfluidics" technology underpins both **Vena Vitals** (Y Combinator S20 follow-on of 2019 Qardiac) and **Makani Science** (FDA-cleared Makani Respiratory Monitor, April 2, 2025).
- Source: <a href="https://engineering.uci.edu/news/2014/7/butterworth-and-beall-competitions-reward-best-both-hardware-and-software">engineering.uci.edu, July 2014</a>.
