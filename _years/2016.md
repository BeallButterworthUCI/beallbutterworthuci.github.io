## Notes

- **Tag Team** is the standout long-tail story of 2016 &mdash; the team's product predated the equivalent Apple AirTag by half a decade.
- **CARGO Therapeutics &rarr; Concentra Biosciences** is the first documented BBComp-traceable M&A exit. CARGO co-founder Louai Labanieh (2016 CeleriBio Beall 3rd lead) went on to become an Assistant Professor at Mount Sinai. The merger agreement was signed July 8, 2025; the tender offer expired August 18, 2025; the merger completed August 19, 2025.
