## Notes

- **Tag Team** is the standout long-tail story of 2016. The team's product predated the equivalent Apple AirTag by half a decade. Two members (Ali Shahbaz and Neeraj Kumar) had also been on the 2015 Beall 2nd Sixth Sense / ECO team.
- **CARGO Therapeutics &rarr; Concentra Biosciences** is the first documented BBComp-traceable M&amp;A exit. CARGO co-founder Louai Labanieh (2016 CeleriBio Beall 3rd lead) went on to become an Assistant Professor at Mount Sinai. The merger agreement was signed July 8, 2025. The tender offer expired August 18, 2025. The merger completed August 19, 2025.
