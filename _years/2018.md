## Notes

- **2018 marked the prize tier step-up** from $7,500 / $5,000 / $2,500 (used 2014-2017) to $10,000 / $6,500 / $3,500 per placement &mdash; the level it has held through 2026.
- **Mehdi Roein-Peikar** (Mechanodontics) went on to complete a three-year orthodontics residency at UCLA in 2019. Education: PhD Biomechanics, Johns Hopkins; MS Quantum Mechanics and Condensed Matter, UIUC; DDS, UCLA School of Dentistry. Won the 2017 UCLA School of Law Lowell Milken Institute-Sandler Prize ($70K).
