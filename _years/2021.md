## Notes

- 2021 was held virtually due to COVID-19.
- **Grasshopper Fund** became one of the first BBComp winners to publicly enter an incubator (UCI Wayfinder) and a national accelerator (Blackstone LaunchPad / Techstars top 30 of 900). Founder **Caitlyn Yang** later joined SpaceX as a Mechanical Engineer.
- The **AllerChain to forMED** trajectory: the 2019 combined-track finalist team (Aguas, McGrale, Shakya, Ananthakrishnan, Agarwal) returned in 2021 with a new product (TonoLase) and won 1st.
- Source: <a href="https://bbcomp.tech.uci.edu/past/past-2021/">bbcomp.tech.uci.edu, 2021</a>.
