## Notes

- Era format: ~10 finalist teams per year, each with a faculty or industry mentor. Final round in Bren Hall. Single-track (software / ICS only).
- Recurring faculty mentors of this era: Don Patterson (Informatics), Ramesh Jain (Bren Chair), Ian Harris (CS).
- Recurring judge: Kevin Kinsey of Netreo, who sat on the 2012 panel and continues today.
- Source: legacy ICS news archive.
