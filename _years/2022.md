## Notes

- 2022 was "back in full swing" per the ICS write-up - the first in-person ceremony after the pandemic. Total purse exceeded $40,000.
- **Leprendo** became the second BBComp winner to operate as an active commercial company; founded 2023 by Dylan Riffle and Dan Ta.
