## Notes

- The **Brazilian Collaboration Prize** debuted in 2023, facilitated by PUCRS associate professor and UCI visiting scholar **Rafael Prikladnicki**. The inaugural cohort: 33 PUCRS students, 40+ UCI students, 17 international teams.
- **Eupnea** went on to win the Microsoft Imagine Cup Epic Challenge 2023 (American Region) and reached the Top 3 worldwide. The Imagine Cup team additionally listed Yogita Mallikarjun Pattan (UCI) and Aditya Sreerama (Duke).
- **Pulsera roster note:** the official engineering.uci.edu 2023 results announcement names only Hsu / Livengood / Fulcomer; Garcia and Bham are listed in the BBComp past-2023 page roster.
