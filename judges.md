<div class="profile">
  <aside class="profile__aside">
    <div class="profile__role">Director since 2014</div>
    <div class="profile__name">David Ochi</div>
    <div class="profile__years">UCI BS '97, MBA '99</div>
  </aside>
  <div class="profile__body">
    <p>Adjunct professor in the Donald Bren School of ICS and the Paul Merage School of Business. Director of the Beall Butterworth Product Design Competition since the inaugural 2014 joint edition. <h2>How the mentor side works</h2>

<p style="max-width: 64ch;">Every BBComp finalist team gets a faculty or industry mentor for the design cycle. In the pre-2014 Butterworth-only era the mentor pool was big enough to assign one per finalist team. The post-2014 joint era moved to a smaller, more curated pool, often with one mentor per winning team plus rotating coaching sessions for all finalists.</p>

<p style="max-width: 64ch;">For the recurring faculty mentors who turn up across multiple cycles (Khine, King, Patterson, Jain, Harris, Nenadic, Kulinsky), see the <a href="{{ '/faculty/' | relative_url }}">faculty mentors page</a>.</p>
