<h2>Founders who appear in both archives</h2>
<p style="max-width: 60ch;">Some BBComp competitors went on to compete at the NVC with different teams. A few crossed over the other way. The full per-person list lives in the underlying data file, but the highest-signal cases are:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Kanu Chandra</strong> placed at the 2022 BBComp with Leprendo and the 2025 BBComp with Oslo. Leprendo also won the 2023 NVC Consumer Services track. He is the most repeat-named UCI competitor in the BBComp record.</li>
  <li><strong>Catlin Tran</strong> placed 2nd in the 2021 BBComp Butterworth track with Nutripair, then won the 2022 NVC Business Products and Services category for the same product.</li>
  <li><strong>Zarina Bahadur</strong> was on the 2018 BBComp Butterworth 1st team (ZOOT), then won the 2020 NVC Grand Prize as the first MIE student to do so, then founded 123 Baby Box.</li>
  <li><strong>Lily McGrale</strong> placed at the 2019 BBComp as a finalist (AllerChain) and the 2021 BBComp Beall 1st (forMED), then took the 2022 NVC Life Sciences category.</li>
  <li><strong>Karan Kanwar</strong> placed 3rd in the 2017 BBComp Butterworth track with Pippy, then won the 2018 NVC Consumer Services category for the same team as Wing Assistant.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why the overlap matters</h2>

<p style="max-width: 64ch;">BBComp asks "did you build it?" The Stella Zhang NVC asks "could it be a business?" Teams that win both in the same cycle have answered both questions inside a single academic year. That is the cleanest proxy we have for a project that crossed the senior-design-to-real-startup line on the schedule we wanted it to.</p>

<p style="max-width: 64ch;">The 2024 cycle is the high-water mark on this metric. All three top BBComp joint-track winners placed in that year's NVC, and the Butterworth 1st-place team (Sentinel) took the overall NVC Grand Prize. That has not happened before or since.</p>

<p style="font-size: 0.9rem; color: var(--ink-500); margin-top: 2rem;">The Stella Zhang NVC has its own historical archive at <a href="https://github.com/StellaZhangUCI/History">StellaZhangUCI/History</a>, structured in the same way as this one.</p>
