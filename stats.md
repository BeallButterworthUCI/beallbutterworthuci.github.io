## The longest-tenured contributors

Eight judges have been on the BBComp panel since the inaugural 2014 joint edition: <strong>Tony Crisp, Kevin Kinsey, Roger Lloyd, Neil Sahota, Jojo Seva, Angela Gribble, Jon Gribble and Mark Logomasini</strong>. David Ochi has directed the joint competition since 2014.

See the <a href="{{ '/judges/' | relative_url }}">judges and mentors page</a> for the full roster.

## Faculty mentor density

Two UCI faculty members have mentored multiple BBComp placements:

- <strong>Prof. Michelle Khine</strong> mentored both 2014 top-2 Beall placements (Spero Diagnostics and A Hundred Tiny Hands), plus the underlying Khine-lab work that became the 2019 Qardiac winner and the Vena Vitals follow-on company. Six BBComp competitors are documented as Khine Lab PhD alumni.
- <strong>Dr. Christine King</strong> mentored three Beall placements in two years: NeoMold (2024 1st), VeinGuard (2025 2nd) and REACH (2025 3rd). The densest two-year run in the archive.

See the <a href="{{ '/faculty/' | relative_url }}">faculty mentors page</a> and the <a href="{{ '/khine-lab/' | relative_url }}">Khine Lab deep dive</a>.
